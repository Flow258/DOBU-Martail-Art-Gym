At DoBu Martial Arts, you will find a warm and friendly atmosphere in which to learn your martial arts of choice, in our high-quality amenities accompanied by well-trained instructors. We offer a large martial arts floor with mats, a complete gym, and other services such as a sauna, steam room, and locker rooms. This very effectively ensures that you make the most of your training experience.

Our Mission

The DoBu Martial Arts is designed to improve the well-being of the inhabitants of our society by offering the best in Martial Arts training. The experienced offering instructors and quality programmatic make sure that the students are provided with the best opportunity to transform into better individuals.

About DoBu Martial Arts

DoBu Martial Arts is dedicated to helping people in our community achieve physical and mental strength through traditional martial arts training. Our experienced instructors and comprehensive programs create an environment where students can grow and excel.

Our History

Mauricio Gomez is the owner and head martial arts coach at DoBu Martial Arts, bringing years of experience and a passion for teaching to the gym. His commitment to fostering a supportive and inclusive environment allows students of all skill levels to thrive. Mauricio emphasizes the importance of discipline, respect, and personal growth in martial arts training. Under his guidance, students not only learn effective techniques but also develop confidence and resilience. His dedication to excellence ensures that every member receives the highest quality instruction and support on their martial arts journey.

With years of experience in martial arts, we've built a strong foundation of trust and excellence in our community. Our commitment to traditional values and modern training methods has helped countless individuals transform their lives.
